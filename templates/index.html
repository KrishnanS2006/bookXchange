About Us

4 High School Students from Northern Virginia recognize college students' struggles to pay for all of the necessities such as textbooks. For this reason, we have started bookXchange in order to allow college students and others to rent out their textbooks to students so that they are able to spend money and time looking for these books.

  • College students are often in debt and do not have the funds to pay for expensive textbooks
  • Some students may have extra books that they are willing to rent and sell for some extra funds
  • For this reason, bookXchange offers a simple and affordable service to rent, buy, and sell textbooks
